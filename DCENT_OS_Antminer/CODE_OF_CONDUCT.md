# Code of Conduct

DCENT_OS is built by D-Central Technologies and the community around it. We want this to be a
place where people who care about open Bitcoin mining can collaborate productively, regardless of
who they are.

## The short version

**Be technical, be honest, be respectful.** Argue about code, not people.

## Expected behavior

- Be welcoming and patient with newcomers — everyone was new to mining hardware once.
- Critique ideas and code, not the person. Keep disagreements technical and evidence-based.
- Assume good faith. Ask questions before assuming malice.
- Respect that **D-Central sets the project's direction** (see [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md)) —
  you can disagree respectfully, and you're always free to fork.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Doxxing or sharing others' private information.
- Deliberately submitting unsafe firmware changes, malicious code, or fake "proof" logs.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions — and in public spaces when
someone is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or block contributors who violate this code.
Report concerns privately to **conduct@d-central.tech** (or **jonathan@d-central.tech**). Reports
are handled confidentially.

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), kept intentionally short.
