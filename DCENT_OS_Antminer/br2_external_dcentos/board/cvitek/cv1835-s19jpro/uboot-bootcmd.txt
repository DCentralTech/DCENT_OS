## Why the former recipe is rejected

The former proposal invented a persistent dcent_boot_count variable, a
dcent_boot_max threshold, and an automatic /config/factory_kernel.bin restore.
Those values are not present vendor behavior. With a BuiltInVolatile
environment they cannot provide persistent boot counting or recovery. The
guessed fw_env.config targeting mmcblk0boot1 was also unsupported and could
overwrite boot material.

The observed selector is instead the contents of GPT p2 at LBA 40960 (0xa000),
length 2048 sectors: zero selects minerfs and nonzero selects upgrade-ramfs.
That fact does not prove a safe write transaction or authorize marker writes.
DCENT_OS has no persistent CV1835 update, recovery, or marker API.
